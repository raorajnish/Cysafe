Report Cyber Crime

If you've been a victim of cyber crime, report it immediately. We'll guide you through the process and connect you with the right authorities.

Emergency Situation?

If you're facing an active cyber attack or urgent threat, contact emergency services immediately.

1930 Cyber Crime
112 Emergency
181 Women Helpline
1098 Child Helpline

How to Report Effectively

1
Immediate Action

Stop any ongoing transactions, change passwords, and secure your accounts immediately.

2
Gather Evidence

Take screenshots, save emails, and collect all relevant proof of the incident.

3
File Complaint

Report on cybercrime.gov.in or call the helpline numbers provided.

4
Follow Up

Keep track of your complaint and provide additional information if needed.

Official Reporting Portals

National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal

cybercrime.gov.in
Public Grievance Portal

pgportal.gov.in
What to Expect
  • You'll receive a complaint number for tracking
  • Investigation may take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity
  • You may be contacted for additional information
  • Keep all evidence safe until case resolution

Need Immediate Assistance?

If you're currently experiencing a cyber attack or have urgent concerns, don't hesitate to contact emergency services immediately.
